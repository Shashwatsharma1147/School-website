School Achievements

 ANNUAL SPORTS DAY
BVN received a certificate of recognition from the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports as FIT INDIA.

On 24th December 2019, BVN celebrated its Annual Sports Day. Mr. Ajay Dutt, the member of Delhi State Assembly, was the chief guest of the event. On this occasion, to commemorate the 150th anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, a grand exhibition Gandhi@150 showcasing beautiful glimpses of bapu’s life exhibition was organized. The theme of the cultural activities was also dedicated to Gandhi’s school of thought depicting his idea of uniting mind, body and spirit in education. Kalaripayatu, Chhau, Tai-chi and Tutting were some of the many techniques that students were trained in to bring bapu’s philosophy of wholesome education to the forefront. The activities ranged from March Past, Yoga, Gymnastics, Dance and Music. The chief guest, Mr. Ajay Dutt Mr. and Mr. Kim Kum Pyoung, Director, Korean Cultural Centre, our special guest applauded the performance of the students and gave away prizes. It was a day filled with sportsmanship, enthusiasm and memories to cherish.

 INDIA-GERMAN STUDENT EXCHANGE PROGRAMME
BVN was adjudged as a Mathematics Promotion School based on the participation in the first and second stages of Aryabhata Ganit Challenge 2019.

India- German student Exchange was conducted at BVN with Giebichenstein-Gymnasium – Thomas Müntzer, Germany. It was a first-hand Inter Cultural Learning Experience for students, teachers and the host families. During their stay five-day stay with us, the German students attended an English role-play and debating classes in school. They also had opportunities to learn Warli and Madhubani Art, do hands-on science experiment, attend Fitness Bhangra and Yoganatyam. The German teachers also had a meaningful dialogue with the French teachers to understand the teaching methodologies practised in school.

 CONNECTING WITH HISTORY
Spinning a charkha

Students of class VI and VII had a unique experience when Ms. Indu Bala from Gandhi Hindustani Sahitya Sabha, Rajghat conducted a workshop on spinning a charkha. This gave them hands-on experience and insight into this art form. Not only did they learn about the importance of Khadi but also the also recalled the teachings of Gandhiji, who advocated the use of Khadi products extensively.

Campus Life

  • Canteen

    Food provided at school has a considerable influence on the development of the children’s long term eating habits, food preferences and attitude towards food. In order to develop healthy eating habits and to encourage social skills, ‘Sampurna’ provides nutritious food for the students. Tiffin is served to the children of classes Nursery to I in the classroom itself which includes a balanced meal consisting of a fruit followed by the main course & the menu is changed everyday to avoid monotony. Canteen is supervised regularly to ensure hygiene while cooking and serving meals.

      

  • Sarala Birla Krida Kendra

    Sarala Birla Krida Kendra is located inside the school campus. Its world class badminton courts and dedicated coach have produced many badminton champions at state, national and international levels. BVN is one of the few schools which has a beautiful and very clean swimming pool, and many great swimmers have been trained here. The Gymnasium under its expert coach has also produced gymnasts at national level. The wooden floored Skating rink is another facet of awe at the sports center. Besides these indoor games, many other sports are also encouraged/played at BVN. To name a few are...Basket ball, Volley Ball, Football, Cricket, Athletics, Chess, Kabaddi, Marshall Arts etc.
